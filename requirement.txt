How to prepare a question paper?
- syllabus
- type of questions(2/7/14 mark questions)
- Higher order thinking questions
- syllabus and SOP has to be uploaded in the software, 
- questions has to be auto generated 
- also need provision to add questions manually
- questions need to be in printable version
- Tag people to review the question paper
- questions has to undergo 3 levels of review
- reviewer should give suggestions on the questions
- after review the status of the question paper has to intimated.
- Validating number of questions and question duplications
- validating mark limits
- grammer check
- sticking to the rules (portions)
- image upload option for questions
- limiting the no. of lines for each question
- provision to upload the question bank(sample questions) to generate question paper
